Assured Cloud Delivery

PA’s proven assured cloud delivery approach ensures successful integration of diverse cloud components to deliver the best overall solution to satisfy business need. We ensure that the real business drivers are understood so the best solution is developed and integrated into full operational capability with the required security.

Features

  • Improved clarity of what the users require from the solution
  • Establish how the solution will be expected to operate
  • Ensure that the solution is robust against misuse
  • Establish a solution architecture and design that maximise the performance
  • Partition the solution delivery between suppliers to minimise their dependencies
  • Monitor supplier design, development and delivery
  • Integrate and test the solution performs as expected before transitioning
  • Performing operational evaluation, validating the solution satisfies user needs
  • Plan decommissioning and disposal of legacy systems

Benefits

  • Timely delivery of complex, integrated cloud and information solutions
  • Establishing solid understanding the real business need before implementation
  • A rigorous approach to ensuring integration of complex solutions
  • Reduction in risk and rework
  • Confidence in solution delivery, timescales and budgets
  • Clients maximise their capability within their programmes and projects

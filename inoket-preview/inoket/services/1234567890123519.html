Behaviour Driven Development (BDD) training for Developers

Behaviour-driven development (BDD) is a process designed to ensure software development meets the unique requirements of your business. This course introduces BDD to developers.

Features

  • Learn how to engage with business to agree acceptance criteria
  • How to use scenarios to guide development processes
  • An introduction to Behat and PHPSpec
  • Adding quality to design process
  • How to automate validation of code against project requirements

Benefits

  • Deliver prioritised features that contribute business value
  • Improve collaboration and communication between technical and non-technical teams
  • Manage workflows effectively; reduce waste and miscommunication

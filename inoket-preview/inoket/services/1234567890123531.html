Skiles, Kautzer and Walker

AWARD Specialist Services

The service is centred on the delivery of expert evaluation, review and decision making processes via the AWARD service, in a procurement context. Services include facilitated criteria development (SCD: Structured Criteria Development), Relative Value for Money (RVfM), weighting workshops, procurement best practice, evaluation strategy and planning, bidder debriefings, AWARD training.

Features

  • AWARD Training
  • Structured Criteria Development (SCD)
  • Procurement best practice and approach
  • Onsite/Remote dedicated support to major projects/programs
  • Relative Value for Money (RVfM)
  • Contract Management/Supplier Performance Review
  • Support for AWARD implementation and rollout across organisations
  • Thought-leadership
  • Red team reviews

Benefits

  • Make the right procurement decision, first time, every time
  • Make best use of AWARD on procurement projects
  • Conduct a robust, auditable and efficient supplier selection
  • Run a Competitive Dialogue in an effective and efficient way
  • Avoid risk of challenge on simple and complex projects
  • Conduct effective post-contract supplier monitoring
  • Meet EU remedies directive
  • Ensure the best value for money decisions
  • Ensure confident procurement decisions are made
